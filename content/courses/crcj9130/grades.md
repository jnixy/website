## Grades 

* Attendance and participation: 30%
* Reaction papers: 25%
* Research proposal: 25%
* Research presentation: 20%

These weighted scores will be added up at the end of the semester to generate your overall grade in the course:

Letter Grade    Score
-------------  -------
A              90 - 100
B              80 - 89
C              70 - 79
D              60 - 69
F              0 - 59
